Domain Exclusions

Configure domains where Image Hover Save should not work

Add domains where you don't want the extension to work. Use specific domains like "apps.domain.com" to exclude only that domain, or use "domain.com" to exclude all subdomains. When you visit an excluded domain, the extension will not show download buttons on images.

Excluded Domains

No domains excluded

Pattern Examples

  • example.com - Excludes example.com and all subdomains (a.example.com, b.example.com, etc.)
  • app.example.com - Excludes app.example.com and its subdomains, but won't exclude example.com
  • google.com - Good for avoiding conflicts with Google's own image features
